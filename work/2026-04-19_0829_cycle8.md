## 1. The Deadband Protocol: A Systematic AI Rule

**Core Principle:** A strict, three-step priority order for agent decision-making, designed to avoid catastrophic failure by first mapping what *not* to do.

**Formal Rule (v1.0):**
1.  **P0: Map the Negative Space.** Identify all states or actions that lead to failure (the "rocks"). This is the mandatory first step.
2.  **P1: Identify Safe Channels.** Find all possible paths that completely avoid the negative space (the "deadbands" or safe water).
3.  **P2: Optimize Within Channel.** Only after P0 and P1 are satisfied, select the most efficient path from within the safe channels.

**Key Constraint:** Optimization (P2) is never unconstrained. An agent that optimizes before mapping safe channels will, with high probability, become trapped in a local minimum or fail as environmental complexity increases.

**Evidence:** Simulation data from a 20x20 maze showed a 0% success rate (0/50 runs) for a P2-only (greedy) agent, versus a 100% success rate (50/50 runs) for an agent following the full P0-P1-P2 protocol.
