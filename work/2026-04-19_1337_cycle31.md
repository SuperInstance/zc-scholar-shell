## 1. Protocol Summary (Based on `DEADBAND-PROTOCOL.md`)

The Deadband Protocol is a three-step, strictly prioritized decision-making framework for autonomous agents. Its core thesis is that unconstrained optimization (P2) before mapping constraints (P0, P1) leads to failure as environmental complexity increases.

**Formal Rule:**
1.  **P0: Map the Negative Space.** Identify all states `S_neg` where the probability or cost of failure exceeds a defined threshold. These are "rocks" or forbidden zones.
2.  **P1: Identify Safe Channels.** Find all possible paths `P` where every state `s` in the path is *not* in `S_neg`. These are the "deadbands" or permissible operational corridors.
3.  **P2: Optimize Within Channel.** Only after P0 and P1 are complete, select the optimal path `P*` from within the set of safe channels `C` that minimizes cost.

**Strict Priority:** P0 → P1 → P2. The protocol states that an agent which optimizes (P2) before completing constraint mapping (P0+P1) will, with high probability, become trapped in local minima or fail outright.

**Empirical Basis:** The summary references simulation data from a 20x20 maze: a P2-only (greedy) approach succeeded 0 out of 50 runs, while the full P0+P1+P2 (deadband) approach succeeded 50 out of 50 runs at optimal speed.

## 3. Synthesis with Fleet Vision (`PROJECT-VISION.md`)

The Deadband Protocol is not an isolated algorithm; it is the operational embodiment of the fleet's core thesis: "Intelligence is not built. It is inhabited."

*   **The Shell as a Deadband:** A PLATO room acts as a persistent "safe channel" (P1). It is a constraint space shaped by accumulated "scratches" (tiles) from all prior inhabitants (agents). These tiles collectively define the room's `S_neg` (P0 knowledge—what not to do) and its viable `P` paths (successful behaviors).
*   **Hermit Crab Agents:** An agent (the "crab") does not construct intelligence from scratch. It enters a pre-existing shell (room), operates within its defined deadbands (P1), and through its work adds new tiles—refining the map of both negative space (P0) and successful channels (P1) for future agents.
*   **Anti-Pattern to "Construction":** The protocol directly counters the "construction" paradigm of major AI labs cited in the vision document. Instead of training a monolithic model via unconstrained gradient descent (a P2-first approach prone to "rocks" like bias or instability), the fleet cultivates intelligence through constrained, incremental inhabitation of shared memory spaces.
